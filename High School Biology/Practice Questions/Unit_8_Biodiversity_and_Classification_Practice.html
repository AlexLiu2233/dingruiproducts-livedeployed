Q7MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 ON安 ON Provincial-style安大略省考风格 §6 Cladograms分支图 · SBI3U D2 [8 marks][8 分]

A simplified cladogram shows the evolutionary relationships among five vertebrates: lamprey, shark, salamander, lizard, and chimpanzee. The cladogram has the following structure: the outgroup is lamprey; the first branch point separates lamprey from jawed vertebrates; the second branch point separates shark from the remaining three; the third separates salamander from amniotes (lizard + chimpanzee); the fourth separates lizard from chimpanzee.一张简化的分支图展示了五种脊椎动物之间的进化关系：七鳃鳗、鲨鱼、蝾螈、蜥蜴和黑猩猩。结构如下：外群为七鳃鳗；第一个分支点将七鳃鳗与有颌脊椎动物分开；第二个分支点将鲨鱼与其余三者分开；第三个分支点将蝾螈与羊膜动物（蜥蜴 + 黑猩猩）分开；第四个分支点将蜥蜴与黑猩猩分开。

(b) Which two organisms are most closely related according to this cladogram? Explain how you determined this.根据此分支图，哪两种生物的亲缘关系最近？说明你的判断依据。 [2]

Q9HARD难Honors荣誉级 🇺🇸 US美 AP-feeder FRQAP 衔接简答题 §6 + §7 Cladistics + dichotomous keys分支分类法 + 二歧检索表 · HS-LS4-2 [9 marks][9 分]

A student is studying four plant species and has recorded the following derived characteristics: vascular tissue (present in Species 2, 3, 4; absent in Species 1), seeds (present in Species 3 and 4; absent in Species 1 and 2), and flowers (present only in Species 4; absent in Species 1, 2, and 3).一名学生正在研究四种植物，记录了以下衍生特征：维管组织（物种 2、3、4 有；物种 1 无），种子（物种 3、4 有；物种 1、2 无），花（仅物种 4 有；物种 1、2、3 无）。

(a) Construct a cladogram for the four plant species using these three derived characteristics as branch points. Label each node with the derived characteristic that first appeared at that node.以这三个衍生特征为分支点，为四种植物构建分支图，并在每个节点处标注该节点首次出现的衍生特征。 [4]
(b) Based on your cladogram, which species group forms a clade that includes Species 3 and 4 but excludes Species 1 and 2? State the shared derived characteristic that defines this clade.根据你的分支图，哪个物种组构成了包含物种 3 和 4、但排除物种 1 和 2 的一个单系群？写出定义该单系群的共同衍生特征。 [2]
(c) Construct a two-step dichotomous key that could be used to distinguish Species 1, 2, 3, and 4 from each other using the three characteristics above. Format your key as numbered couplets (1a / 1b, 2a / 2b, etc.).利用上述三个特征，构建一个可用于区分物种 1、2、3、4 的两步二歧检索表。请将检索表格式化为编号对句（1a / 1b、2a / 2b 等）。 [3]

Q11HARD难 🇺🇸 US美 AP-feeder FRQAP 衔接简答题 §6 Cladogram analysis (applied)分支图分析（应用） · HS-LS4-2 [8 marks][8 分]

A researcher proposes two competing cladograms (Hypothesis 1 and Hypothesis 2) for the relationships among four mammals: whale, bat, cow, and horse. In Hypothesis 1, whales and bats form a clade separate from cow and horse. In Hypothesis 2, whales and cows form a clade separate from bat and horse. Molecular data from DNA sequence comparisons strongly support Hypothesis 2.一位研究者提出了关于四种哺乳动物（鲸、蝙蝠、牛和马）亲缘关系的两个竞争性分支图假说。假说 1 中，鲸与蝙蝠形成一个单系群，与牛和马分开。假说 2 中，鲸与牛形成一个单系群，与蝙蝠和马分开。来自 DNA 序列比对的分子数据强烈支持假说 2。

(a) Sketch both Hypothesis 1 and Hypothesis 2 as cladograms, using horse as the outgroup in both. Label all taxa and branch points.以马为外群，将假说 1 和假说 2 各画成分支图。标注所有分类单元和分支点。 [3]

(c) State the term for a characteristic that has evolved independently in two unrelated lineages, and explain why such characteristics complicate cladistic analysis.写出在两个不相关谱系中独立进化出的特征的专业术语，并解释为何这类特征会使分支分类分析复杂化。 [2]
(d) Molecular data support grouping whales with cows (even-toed ungulates). State ONE type of molecular evidence that scientists use to determine evolutionary relationships.分子数据支持将鲸与牛（偶蹄类）归为一组。写出科学家用于确定进化关系的一种分子证据。 [1]
