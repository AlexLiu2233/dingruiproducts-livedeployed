Section A · Short ResponseA 部分 · 短答题

For short-answer items, write balanced equations with state symbols where asked. A student mixes two clear, colourless solutions. Which observation provides the STRONGEST evidence that a chemical reaction has occurred?学生将两种透明无色溶液混合。以下哪种观察现象为化学反应发生提供了最有力的证据？

  1. (A) The mixture feels slightly warm混合物感觉略微变热
  2. (B) A yellow solid forms that cannot be filtered back into either original solution形成黄色固体，无法过滤还原为任一原溶液
  3. (C) The volume of liquid increases液体体积增大
  4. (D) The solution turns cloudy then clears after stirring溶液变混浊后搅拌后变清

Q3EASY易 🇨🇦 BC卑 BC Provincial-style卑诗省考风格 §3 Synthesis and decomposition化合与分解 · Science 10 [5 marks][5 分]

Calcium carbonate, $\text{CaCO}_3$, decomposes when strongly heated to form two products.碳酸钙 $\text{CaCO}_3$ 在强热下分解生成两种产物。

(a) Write the balanced equation for this decomposition, including state symbols.书写该分解反应的配平方程式，包括状态符号。 [2]
(b) Name the two products formed.命名生成的两种产物。 [2]

Q4MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 AB阿 AB Diploma-style阿尔伯塔毕业考风格 §4 Single replacement单置换 · Chem 20 D GO1 [6 marks][6 分]

Zinc metal is placed into a solution of copper(II) sulfate, $\text{CuSO}_4\text{(aq)}$. A red-brown solid forms on the zinc and the blue colour of the solution fades.锌金属放入硫酸铜 $\text{CuSO}_4\text{(aq)}$ 溶液中。锌上形成红棕色固体，溶液的蓝色逐渐褪去。

(a) Write the balanced molecular equation for this reaction, with state symbols.书写该反应的配平分子方程式，含状态符号。 [2]
(b) Classify this reaction type and state the general pattern.确定该反应类型，并写出其通式。 [2]
(c) Explain, using the activity series, why zinc displaces copper but copper would NOT displace zinc.利用活动性顺序解释为何锌能置换铜，而铜不能置换锌。 [2]
Q5MEDIUM中 🇺🇸 US美 AP-style MCQAP 风格选择题 §5 Combustion燃烧 · HS-PS1-7 [8 marks][8 分]

Propane, $\text{C}_3\text{H}_8$, undergoes complete combustion in excess oxygen.丙烷 $\text{C}_3\text{H}_8$ 在过量氧气中完全燃烧。

(a) Write the balanced equation for the complete combustion of propane, with state symbols (assume gaseous water).书写丙烷完全燃烧的配平方程式，含状态符号（假设水为气态）。 [3]
(b) State the two products of complete combustion of any hydrocarbon.写出任何烃类完全燃烧的两种产物。 [2]
(c) Name one product that forms if combustion is incomplete, and explain why this is an environmental concern.写出不完全燃烧时生成的一种产物，并解释为何这引发环境问题。 [3]

Section B · Extended ResponseB 部分 · 简答题

Show every step of reasoning. Write balanced equations including state symbols in every final answer. For classification questions, name the reaction type AND state the general pattern (A + B → AB, etc.). Calculator permitted throughout Part II.每一步推理都要写出。每个最终方程式答案须配平并含状态符号。分类题需命名反应类型并写出通式（A + B → AB 等）。第二部分全程可用计算器。

Q7MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 ON安 ON Provincial-style安大略省考风格 §6 Solubility and precipitation溶解度与沉淀 · SCH3U C3.2 [8 marks][8 分]

An aqueous solution of lead(II) nitrate, $\text{Pb(NO}_3)_2\text{(aq)}$, is mixed with an aqueous solution of potassium iodide, $\text{KI(aq)}$. A bright yellow precipitate forms immediately.硝酸铅 $\text{Pb(NO}_3)_2\text{(aq)}$ 水溶液与碘化钾 $\text{KI(aq)}$ 水溶液混合，立即生成亮黄色沉淀。

(a) Identify the precipitate by name and formula, using solubility rules to justify your choice.用溶解度规则命名并写出沉淀的化学式，并说明理由。 [2]

(c) Write the complete ionic equation.书写完全离子方程式。 [2]
(d) Identify the spectator ions and write the net ionic equation.确定旁观离子，并书写净离子方程式。 [2]

Q10MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 AB阿 AB Diploma-style阿尔伯塔毕业考风格 §3 + §5 Reaction types (applied)反应类型（应用） · Chem 20 D GO1 [8 marks][8 分]

A chemistry student observes four different reactions in lab. For each description, (i) classify the reaction type and (ii) write the balanced equation with state symbols.化学实验中，一名学生观察了四种不同的反应。对于每种描述，(i) 确定反应类型，(ii) 书写带状态符号的配平方程式。

(a) Magnesium ribbon burns in oxygen gas to form a white powder, magnesium oxide.镁带在氧气中燃烧，生成白色粉末氧化镁。 [2]
(b) Hydrogen peroxide solution slowly decomposes into water and oxygen gas.过氧化氢溶液缓慢分解生成水和氧气。 [2]
(c) Iron metal is placed in copper(II) sulfate solution; a red-brown solid deposits on the iron.铁金属放入硫酸铜溶液中，铁上沉积出红棕色固体。 [2]
(d) Ethane, $\text{C}_2\text{H}_6$, burns completely in excess oxygen.乙烷 $\text{C}_2\text{H}_6$ 在过量氧气中完全燃烧。 [2]
Q11MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 ON安 🇨🇦 AB阿 AP-feeder FRQAP 衔接简答题 §6 + §7 Precipitation and net ionic沉淀与净离子 · SCH3U C3.2 [9 marks][9 分]

A water-treatment facility uses a precipitation reaction to remove calcium ions from hard water. A technician adds sodium carbonate solution, $\text{Na}_2\text{CO}_3\text{(aq)}$, to a sample of hard water containing dissolved calcium chloride, $\text{CaCl}_2\text{(aq)}$.一家水处理厂通过沉淀反应去除硬水中的钙离子。技术员向含有溶解氯化钙 $\text{CaCl}_2\text{(aq)}$ 的硬水样品中加入碳酸钠溶液 $\text{Na}_2\text{CO}_3\text{(aq)}$。

(a) Use solubility rules to predict whether a precipitate forms, and name it.利用溶解度规则预测是否生成沉淀，并命名。 [2]
(b) Write the balanced molecular equation with state symbols.书写带状态符号的配平分子方程式。 [2]
(c) Write the complete ionic equation.书写完全离子方程式。 [2]
(d) Write the net ionic equation.书写净离子方程式。 [1]
(e) Explain in one sentence how this precipitation reaction achieves water softening.用一句话解释沉淀反应如何实现软化水处理。 [2]
Q12HARD难 🇺🇸 US美 🇨🇦 AB阿 AP-feeder FRQAP 衔接简答题 §1-7 Cross-topic investigation跨题型综合探究 · HS-PS1-2 · Chem 20 D GO3 [9 marks][9 分]

A student conducts three experiments. Read each description carefully and answer all parts.学生进行了三个实验。仔细阅读每个描述并回答所有小题。

(a) Experiment 1: Hydrogen gas is burned in a limited supply of oxygen, producing only water. Write the balanced equation. Is this a synthesis or combustion reaction? Justify your answer.实验 1：氢气在有限供应的氧气中燃烧，仅生成水。书写配平方程式。这是化合反应还是燃烧反应？说明理由。 [3]
(b) Experiment 2: Copper(II) chloride solution, $\text{CuCl}_2\text{(aq)}$, is mixed with sodium hydroxide solution, $\text{NaOH(aq)}$. A blue precipitate forms. Write the molecular, complete ionic, and net ionic equations. Use solubility rules to identify the precipitate.实验 2：氯化铜溶液 $\text{CuCl}_2\text{(aq)}$ 与氢氧化钠溶液 $\text{NaOH(aq)}$ 混合，生成蓝色沉淀。书写分子方程式、完全离子方程式和净离子方程式。利用溶解度规则确定沉淀。 [4]
(c) Experiment 3: A strip of silver metal is placed in a solution of copper(II) nitrate. Predict whether a reaction occurs; use the activity series to justify. If a reaction occurs, write the balanced equation; if not, write NR.实验 3：将一条银金属条放入硝酸铜溶液中。预测是否发生反应；利用活动性顺序说明理由。若发生反应，书写配平方程式；若不反应，写 NR。 [2]
