Q2EASY易 🇨🇦 ON安 ON Provincial-style安大略省考风格 §2 Trophic levels营养级 · SBI3U E1 [4 marks][4 分]

Consider the food chain: Grass → Grasshopper → Frog → Snake → Hawk.考虑食物链：草 → 蚱蜢 → 青蛙 → 蛇 → 鹰。

(a) Name the producer and identify its trophic level.写出生产者并指出其营养级。 [1]
(b) Identify the primary consumer. Explain what "primary consumer" means in terms of energy source.写出初级消费者。用能量来源解释"初级消费者"的含义。 [2]
(c) State at which trophic level the snake feeds.说明蛇处于哪个营养级。 [1]
Q3MEDIUM中 🇺🇸 US美 AP-style MCQAP 风格选择题 §2 10% energy rule10% 能量法则 · HS-LS2-4 [3 marks][3 分]

An ecosystem's producers fix $800{,}000$ kJ of energy per year. Applying the 10% energy-transfer rule, how much energy is available to secondary consumers?某生态系统的生产者每年固定 $800{,}000$ kJ 能量。按 10% 能量传递法则，次级消费者可获得多少能量？

  1. (A) $80{,}000\ \text{kJ}$
  2. (B) $8{,}000\ \text{kJ}$
  3. (C) $800\ \text{kJ}$
  4. (D) $80\ \text{kJ}$
Q4MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 BC卑 BC Provincial-style卑诗省考风格 §3 Carbon cycle碳循环 · Biology 12 [6 marks][6 分]

The carbon cycle moves carbon through biotic and abiotic components of ecosystems.碳循环使碳在生态系统的生物和非生物组分之间流动。

(a) Name the process by which producers remove $\text{CO}_2$ from the atmosphere and state where it occurs in the cell.写出生产者从大气中移除 $\text{CO}_2$ 的过程名称，并说明该过程发生在细胞的哪个部位。 [2]
(b) Name two processes that return carbon to the atmosphere as $\text{CO}_2$.写出两个将碳以 $\text{CO}_2$ 形式释放回大气的过程。 [2]
(c) Explain why burning fossil fuels disrupts the natural carbon cycle.解释为什么燃烧化石燃料会扰乱自然碳循环。 [2]
Q5MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 AB阿 AB Diploma-style阿尔伯塔毕业考风格 §4 Population dynamics种群动态 · Biology 30 D2 [9 marks][9 分]

A rabbit population in a meadow is studied over several years. Initially the population grows rapidly, then levels off near a stable size of 400 individuals.研究人员多年观测一处草地的兔子种群。起初种群快速增长，之后稳定在约 400 只。

(a) Identify the type of population growth described. Sketch the expected population-size vs. time curve and label the inflection point.判断上述种群增长的类型。画出预期的种群数量随时间变化的曲线，并标出拐点。 [3]
(b) State the term for the stable size of 400 individuals and identify two biotic factors that could limit the population at this size.写出"400 只"这一稳定数量的术语，并举出两个可将种群限制在此数量的生物因素。 [3]
(c) Distinguish between density-dependent and density-independent limiting factors. Give one example of each.区分密度制约因素和非密度制约因素，各举一例。 [3]

Q6EASY易 🇺🇸 US美 AP-feeder FRQAP 衔接简答题 §5 Community interactions群落互作 · HS-LS2-2 [6 marks][6 分]

Symbiotic relationships are close, long-term interactions between two species.共生关系是两个物种之间密切的长期互作。

(a) Define mutualism and give one named example, identifying both species and the benefit each gains.定义互利共生，并举一个具体例子，写明两个物种及各自获得的利益。 [3]
(b) Compare parasitism and commensalism: for each, state the effect on the host/companion species (benefit, harm, or no effect).比较寄生和片利共生：分别说明对宿主/同伴物种的影响（受益、受害或无影响）。 [3]
Q7MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 ON安 ON Provincial-style安大略省考风格 §6 Ecological succession生态演替 · SBI3U E3 [8 marks][8 分]

After a volcanic eruption destroys all life on bare rock, organisms gradually colonize the area over centuries.火山爆发将裸岩上的生命全部摧毁后，生物在数百年间逐渐重新定殖该区域。

(a) Identify the type of succession described above and explain why it differs from secondary succession.判断上述演替类型，并解释其与次生演替的区别。 [3]
(b) List the expected sequence of pioneer, intermediate, and climax communities in a temperate region, giving one representative organism for each stage.列出温带地区先锋群落、中间群落和顶极群落的预期演替顺序，每个阶段各举一种代表性生物。 [3]
(c) Explain how pioneer species facilitate the arrival of later species.解释先锋物种如何促进后续物种的到来。 [2]
Q8MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 AB阿 AB Diploma-style阿尔伯塔毕业考风格 §3 Nitrogen cycle氮循环 · Biology 30 D1 [8 marks][8 分]

Nitrogen is an essential element for all living organisms but most cannot use atmospheric $\text{N}_2$ directly.氮是所有生物必需的元素，但大多数生物不能直接利用大气中的 $\text{N}_2$。

(a) Define nitrogen fixation and name one type of organism that carries out this process.定义氮固定，并写出一种执行该过程的生物类型。 [2]
(b) Describe the role of decomposers in the nitrogen cycle. Name the process that converts organic nitrogen in dead matter back to ammonium ($\text{NH}_4^+$).描述分解者在氮循环中的作用。写出将死亡物质中有机氮转化回铵 ($\text{NH}_4^+$) 的过程名称。 [3]
(c) Explain how denitrification completes the nitrogen cycle and state the product returned to the atmosphere.解释反硝化作用如何完成氮循环，并写出返回大气的产物。 [3]
Q9HARD难Honors荣誉级 🇺🇸 US美 AP-feeder FRQAP 衔接简答题 §2 Energy pyramids (quantitative)能量金字塔（定量） · HS-LS2-4 [8 marks][8 分]

In a grassland ecosystem, producers capture $5{,}000{,}000$ kJ of solar energy per year. Assume the ecological efficiency between each trophic level is exactly 10%.在某草原生态系统中，生产者每年捕获 $5{,}000{,}000$ kJ 太阳能。假设各营养级之间的生态效率恰好为 10%。

(a) Calculate the energy available at each of the four trophic levels (producers through tertiary consumers). Show your working for each step.计算四个营养级（生产者至三级消费者）各自可获得的能量。每步都要写出计算过程。 [4]
(b) Calculate what percentage of the producers' original energy reaches the tertiary consumers.计算生产者原始能量中有多大比例到达三级消费者。 [2]
(c) Explain why food chains rarely exceed five trophic levels, using the 10% rule as part of your answer.用 10% 法则解释为什么食物链很少超过五个营养级。 [2]

Q10MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 AB阿 AB Diploma-style阿尔伯塔毕业考风格 §5 Predator-prey dynamics捕食者-猎物动态 · Biology 30 D3 [8 marks][8 分]

Scientists monitor a wolf-moose system in a boreal forest. Over 20 years, the data show classic oscillating cycles: when moose numbers peak, wolf numbers increase shortly after; when wolves peak, moose numbers fall.科学家在北方森林监测一个狼-驼鹿系统。20 年数据显示经典振荡周期：驼鹿数量达峰后不久狼数量增加；狼数量达峰后驼鹿数量下降。

(a) Explain why wolf and moose populations cycle out of phase with each other rather than peaking at the same time.解释为什么狼和驼鹿种群的峰值不同步，而不是同时达到峰值。 [3]
(b) Predict what would happen to the moose population if wolves were removed from the ecosystem. Include reference to carrying capacity and vegetation.预测如果狼从生态系统中被移除，驼鹿种群会发生什么变化。请提及承载量和植被。 [3]
(c) Classify the wolf-moose relationship and explain one way wolves may also benefit moose populations indirectly over time.判断狼-驼鹿关系的类型，并解释狼在长期内可能间接使驼鹿种群受益的一种方式。 [2]
Q11MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 BC卑 BC Provincial-style卑诗省考风格 §7 Human impact and sustainability人类影响与可持续性 · HS-LS2-6 [8 marks][8 分]

Eutrophication is a process caused primarily by nutrient runoff from agricultural land into lakes and rivers.富营养化主要由农业用地的营养物质径流进入湖泊和河流引起。

(a) Describe the sequence of events that leads from excess nitrate and phosphate entering a lake to a "dead zone" with very low oxygen levels. Include the role of decomposers in your answer.描述多余硝酸盐和磷酸盐进入湖泊后，直至形成氧含量极低的"死区"的一系列事件。答案中须包含分解者的作用。 [4]
(b) Propose two strategies that farmers could adopt to reduce nutrient runoff, and explain the mechanism by which each strategy works.提出农民可采用的两种减少营养物质径流的策略，并解释每种策略的作用机制。 [4]
Q12HARD难 🇺🇸 US美 AP-feeder FRQAP 衔接简答题 §1 + §2 + §7 Ecosystem analysis生态系统综合分析 · HS-LS2-2 [10 marks][10 分]

A kelp forest ecosystem contains the following species: sea urchins (herbivores), sea otters (predators of sea urchins), kelp (producer), rockfish (omnivores), and decomposer bacteria. Human hunting nearly eliminated sea otters from this ecosystem.一个海带森林生态系统包含以下物种：海胆（草食动物）、海獭（海胆的天敌）、海带（生产者）、石斑鱼（杂食动物）和分解菌。人类猎杀使海獭几乎从该生态系统中消失。

(a) Construct a food web using all five groups named above. Indicate the direction of energy flow with arrows.用上述五个类群构建一个食物网。用箭头标示能量流动方向。 [3]
(b) Predict and explain the cascading effects on kelp abundance when sea otter numbers declined. Use the term "trophic cascade" in your answer.预测并解释海獭数量减少时对海带数量的级联影响。答案中须使用"营养级联"这一术语。 [4]
(c) Sea otters are considered a keystone species. Define keystone species and justify this classification for sea otters based on the food web evidence above.海獭被视为关键物种。定义关键物种，并根据上述食物网证据说明为何海獭符合这一定义。 [3]
