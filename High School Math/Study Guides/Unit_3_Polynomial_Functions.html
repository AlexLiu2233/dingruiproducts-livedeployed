A polynomial in $x$.$x$ 的多项式。 Finite sum of $a_{k} x^{k}$ with $k$ a non-negative integer:$a_{k} x^{k}$（$k$ 为非负整数）的有限和： $$ p(x) \;=\; a_{n} x^{n} + a_{n-1} x^{n-1} + \cdots + a_{1} x + a_{0}, \qquad a_{n} \ne 0. $$
  • Degree次数 $n$ , largest exponent with non-zero coefficient (linear $1$, quadratic $2$, cubic $3$, quartic $4$, quintic $5$)., 非零系数对应的最高次（一次 $1$、二次 $2$、三次 $3$、四次 $4$、五次 $5$）。
  • Leading coefficient首项系数 $a_{n}$ , controls end behaviour., 决定末端行为。
  • Constant term常数项 $a_{0} = p(0)$ , the $y$-intercept., 即 $y$ 截距。
  • Monic首一 , leading coefficient $1$. Terms vs. factors: terms are added, factors are multiplied., 首项系数为 $1$。项与因式：项是相加的，因式是相乘的。
End behaviour.末端行为。 Only $a_{n} x^{n}$ matters as $x \to \pm \infty$. Four cases by parity of $n$ and sign of $a_{n}$: even-positive both ends to $+\infty$ (like $x^{2}$); even-negative both to $-\infty$; odd-positive left $\to -\infty$, right $\to +\infty$ (like $x^{3}$); odd-negative the reverse.当 $x \to \pm \infty$ 时只看 $a_{n} x^{n}$。按 $n$ 的奇偶和 $a_{n}$ 的符号分四种：偶 + 正两端均趋 $+\infty$（似 $x^{2}$）；偶 + 负两端均趋 $-\infty$；奇 + 正左 $\to -\infty$、右 $\to +\infty$（似 $x^{3}$）；奇 + 负相反。

Factor $a_{n} x^{n}$ out: $p(x) = a_{n} x^{n} \left( 1 + \frac{a_{n-1}}{a_{n} x} + \cdots + \frac{a_{0}}{a_{n} x^{n}} \right)$. Section 2 · Arithmetic and division第 2 节 · 运算与除法

Polynomial Arithmetic and Division多项式运算与除法

Addition and multiplication.加法与乘法。 Add by combining like terms (same exponent). Multiply by the distributive law term-by-term; the degree of a product is the sum of the degrees.相加合并同类项（同指数）；按分配律逐项相乘；乘积的次数等于各因式次数之和。

Long division.长除法。 To divide $p(x)$ by $d(x)$:用 $d(x)$ 除 $p(x)$：
  1. Arrange both in descending order, including a $0$ coefficient for any missing power.两者均按降幂排列，缺项用 $0$ 系数补齐。
  2. Divide the leading term of $p$ by the leading term of $d$ to get the next term of the quotient.$p$ 的首项除以 $d$ 的首项，得到商的下一项。
  3. Multiply $d$ by that term and subtract from $p$.用该项乘 $d$，从 $p$ 中减去。
  4. Repeat with the new "remainder so far" until its degree is less than $\deg d$.对新的"当前余数"重复以上步骤，直到其次数小于 $\deg d$。
The result is $p(x) = d(x) \cdot q(x) + r(x)$ with $\deg r < \deg d$.最终 $p(x) = d(x) \cdot q(x) + r(x)$，且 $\deg r < \deg d$。

Synthetic division.综合除法。 A streamlined shortcut for dividing by a linear factor $(x - a)$:除以线性因式 $(x - a)$ 的速记法：
  1. Write the coefficients of $p$ in descending order (use $0$ for missing powers).按降幂写出 $p$ 的系数（缺项补 $0$）。
  2. Put $a$ on the left.把 $a$ 放在左侧。
  3. Bring down the first coefficient. Multiply by $a$ and add to the next coefficient. Repeat.把首系数下移；乘 $a$ 加到下一系数；如此循环。
  4. The final number is $p(a)$ , the remainder. The other numbers, in order, are the coefficients of the quotient (degree one less than $p$).最后一个数即 $p(a)$ , 余数；其余数字按序为商的系数（次数比 $p$ 低 $1$）。
Synthetic division is exactly long division by $(x - a)$, rewritten as a number-crunching procedure.综合除法本质上就是除以 $(x - a)$ 的长除法，写成纯数字流程而已。

Going deeper · Why the remainder theorem is true深入 · 余数定理为何成立

Long division of $p(x)$ by $(x - a)$ leaves a remainder $r(x)$ with $\deg r < \deg(x - a) = 1$, so $r$ is a constant: $p(x) = (x - a) q(x) + r$. Substitute $x = a$: $p(a) = 0 \cdot q(a) + r = r$. So the remainder equals $p(a)$ , the remainder theorem in one substitution. The factor theorem is the special case $p(a) = 0$: then $p(x) = (x - a) q(x)$, exhibiting $(x - a)$ as a factor; the converse is immediate.$p(x)$ 长除 $(x - a)$ 得到余式 $r(x)$，且 $\deg r < \deg(x - a) = 1$，故 $r$ 是常数：$p(x) = (x - a) q(x) + r$。代入 $x = a$：$p(a) = 0 \cdot q(a) + r = r$。故余数等于 $p(a)$ , 一次代入即得余数定理。因式定理是 $p(a) = 0$ 的特例：此时 $p(x) = (x - a) q(x)$，$(x - a)$ 显式为因式；反向亦显然。

$$ a_{n} p^{n} + a_{n-1} p^{n-1} q + \cdots + a_{1} p q^{n-1} + a_{0} q^{n} \;=\; 0. $$

Every term except the last is divisible by $p$, so $p \mid a_{0} q^{n}$; since $\gcd(p, q) = 1$, this forces $p \mid a_{0}$. If $p(x) = a_{n} x^{n} + \cdots + a_{0}$ has real coefficients (so $\bar{a}_{k} = a_{k}$) and $p(z) = 0$, apply conjugation to both sides: $\overline{p(z)} = a_{n} \bar{z}^{n} + \cdots + a_{0} = p(\bar{z})$, while the left side is $\overline{0} = 0$. Common Questions常见问题

What is the factor theorem?什么是因式定理？

The factor theorem says that (x - c) is a factor of a polynomial exactly when substituting x = c makes the polynomial equal zero, so c is a root.因式定理指出，当代入 x = c 使多项式等于零时，(x - c) 恰好是该多项式的一个因式，因此 c 是一个根。

How do I find the rational roots of a polynomial?如何求多项式的有理根？

The rational root theorem lists candidate roots as factors of the constant term over factors of the leading coefficient; test each with synthetic or direct substitution.有理根定理把候选根列为常数项的因数除以首项系数的因数，再用综合除法或直接代入逐一检验。

How does the degree affect the end behavior of a polynomial graph?次数如何影响多项式图像的末端走向？

The leading term sets the end behavior: even degree sends both ends the same direction, odd degree sends them opposite ways, and the leading sign decides up or down.首项决定末端走向：偶次时两端走向相同，奇次时两端走向相反，首项系数的正负决定向上还是向下。

Memorise four facts. (1) The factor theorem: $p(a) = 0$ if and only if $(x - a)$ is a factor of $p(x)$. (2) The remainder theorem: the remainder when $p(x)$ is divided by $(x - a)$ is $p(a)$. (3) End behaviour is set by the leading term: $a_{n} x^{n}$ alone decides what happens as $x \to \pm \infty$. (4) A polynomial of degree $n$ has at most $n - 1$ turning points and exactly $n$ roots counted with multiplicity (in $\mathbb{C}$).
