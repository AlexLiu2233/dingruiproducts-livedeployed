Q1EASY易 🇺🇸 US美 AP-style MCQAP 风格选择题 §1 Work done by a force力所做的功 · HS-PS3-1 [3 marks][3 分]

A crate is pushed $4.0$ m across a level floor by a constant horizontal force of $50$ N. How much work does the pushing force do on the crate?一个箱子被一个 $50$ N 的恒定水平力沿水平地面推动 $4.0$ m。推力对箱子做了多少功？

  1. (A) $12.5\ \text{J}$
  2. (B) $200\ \text{J}$
  3. (C) $0\ \text{J}$
  4. (D) $54\ \text{J}$
Q2EASY易 🇺🇸 US美 AP-style MCQAP 风格选择题 §2 Kinetic energy动能 · HS-PS3-2 [3 marks][3 分]

A $2.0$ kg ball moves with a speed of $6.0$ m/s. What is its kinetic energy?一个 $2.0$ kg 的球以 $6.0$ m/s 的速率运动。它的动能是多少？

  1. (A) $12\ \text{J}$
  2. (B) $36\ \text{J}$
  3. (C) $72\ \text{J}$
  4. (D) $6.0\ \text{J}$
Q3EASY易 🇨🇦 ON安 ON Provincial-style安大略省考风格 §3 Potential energy势能 · SPH3U D3.2 [4 marks][4 分]

Use $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$.取 $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$。

(a) A $4.0$ kg textbook is lifted $2.0$ m from the floor to a shelf. Compute the gain in gravitational potential energy, with units.一本 $4.0$ kg 的教科书从地面被抬到架子上，升高 $2.0$ m。计算重力势能的增量，并写出单位。 [2]
(b) A spring of constant $k = 300$ N/m is compressed by $0.20$ m. Compute the elastic potential energy stored.一根劲度系数 $k = 300$ N/m 的弹簧被压缩 $0.20$ m。计算其储存的弹性势能。 [1]
(c) State why a reference level must be chosen before a value of gravitational potential energy is meaningful.说明为何在重力势能的数值有意义之前必须先选定参考面。 [1]

Q5MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 BC卑 BC Provincial-style卑诗省考风格 §4 Conservation of energy机械能守恒 · Physics 11 [5 marks][5 分]

A $0.30$ kg ball is dropped from rest at a height of $1.8$ m above the ground. Ignore air resistance and use $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$.一个 $0.30$ kg 的球从距地面 $1.8$ m 处由静止落下。忽略空气阻力，取 $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$。

(a) Using energy conservation, find the speed of the ball just before it hits the ground.用能量守恒，求球刚落地前的速率。 [2]
(b) State the kinetic energy of the ball at the instant of impact.写出球落地瞬间的动能。 [2]
(c) State the condition that allows you to set initial mechanical energy equal to final mechanical energy.写出允许你将初机械能等于末机械能的条件。 [1]

Q6MEDIUM中 🇺🇸 US美 AP-feeder FRQAP 衔接简答题 §2 Work-energy theorem功能定理 · HS-PS3-2 [8 marks][8 分]

A $1000$ kg car travelling at $20$ m/s brakes and comes to a complete stop in a distance of $50$ m. The road is level.一辆 $1000$ kg 的汽车以 $20$ m/s 行驶，刹车后在 $50$ m 内完全停下。路面水平。

(a) Find the initial kinetic energy of the car.求汽车的初始动能。 [2]
(b) Using the work-energy theorem, find the net work done on the car while braking.用功能定理，求刹车过程中对汽车做的合功。 [2]
(c) Find the magnitude of the (constant) braking force.求（恒定）刹车力的大小。 [2]
(d) If the car had instead been travelling at $40$ m/s with the same braking force, by what factor would the braking distance change? Justify with the work-energy theorem.若汽车改以 $40$ m/s 行驶、刹车力不变，刹车距离会变为原来的几倍？用功能定理论证。 [2]
Q7MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 ON安 ON Provincial-style安大略省考风格 §4 Conservation of mechanical energy机械能守恒 · SPH3U D3.1 [8 marks][8 分]

A $50$ kg child starts from rest at the top of a smooth (frictionless) playground slide. The top of the slide is $4.0$ m above the bottom. Take $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$ and take the bottom as the reference level.一个 $50$ kg 的孩子从一个光滑（无摩擦）滑梯顶端静止出发。滑梯顶端高于底端 $4.0$ m。取 $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$，以底端为参考面。

(a) Find the gravitational potential energy of the child at the top.求孩子在顶端的重力势能。 [2]
(b) Find the speed of the child at the bottom of the slide.求孩子到达滑梯底端的速率。 [3]
(c) Find the speed of the child at the point on the slide that is $1.5$ m above the bottom.求孩子在滑梯上距底端 $1.5$ m 处的速率。 [2]
(d) Explain why the speed at the bottom does not depend on the mass of the child.解释为何底端速率与孩子的质量无关。 [1]
Q8HARD难 🇨🇦 BC卑 BC Provincial-style卑诗省考风格 §6 Non-conservative forces非保守力 · Physics 11 [9 marks][9 分]

A $2.0$ kg block starts from rest and slides down a ramp whose top is $3.0$ m above the bottom. A constant kinetic friction force of $5.0$ N acts along the entire $5.0$ m path down the ramp. Take $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$ and the bottom as the reference level.一个 $2.0$ kg 的滑块从静止沿斜坡下滑，坡顶高于坡底 $3.0$ m。沿整段 $5.0$ m 的下坡路径作用着 $5.0$ N 的恒定动摩擦力。取 $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$，以坡底为参考面。

(a) Find the gravitational potential energy of the block at the top.求滑块在坡顶的重力势能。 [2]
(b) Find the work done by friction over the descent.求下坡过程中摩擦力所做的功。 [2]
(c) Using the extended energy equation $KE_f + PE_f = KE_i + PE_i + W_\text{nc}$, find the kinetic energy of the block at the bottom.用扩展能量方程 $KE_f + PE_f = KE_i + PE_i + W_\text{nc}$，求滑块到达坡底的动能。 [2]
(d) Find the speed of the block at the bottom.求滑块到达坡底的速率。 [2]
(e) State how much thermal energy was generated, and where it came from.写出产生了多少热能，以及它来自何处。 [1]
Q9HARD难Honors荣誉级 🇺🇸 US美 AP-feeder FRQAP 衔接简答题 §3 + §4 Spring energy & conservation弹性能与守恒 · HS-PS3-1 [10 marks][10 分]

A spring of constant $k = 800$ N/m is compressed by $0.30$ m and used to launch a $1.5$ kg block along a frictionless horizontal surface, which then leads smoothly into a frictionless incline. Take $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$.一根劲度系数 $k = 800$ N/m 的弹簧被压缩 $0.30$ m，用来在无摩擦的水平面上发射一个 $1.5$ kg 的滑块，该水平面平滑地接入一个无摩擦斜面。取 $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$。

(a) Find the elastic potential energy stored in the compressed spring.求压缩弹簧中储存的弹性势能。 [2]
(b) Find the speed of the block as it leaves the spring on the flat surface.求滑块在水平面上离开弹簧时的速率。 [2]
(c) Find the maximum vertical height the block rises to on the frictionless incline.求滑块在无摩擦斜面上上升的最大竖直高度。 [2]
(d) Suppose instead a rough patch on the flat section does $-9.0$ J of work on the block before it reaches the incline. Find the block's speed at the base of the incline.现假设水平段上一处粗糙区域在滑块到达斜面前对它做了 $-9.0$ J 的功。求滑块在斜面底部的速率。 [2]
(e) For the fully frictionless case, use an energy audit to explain why the block returns to the spring with the same speed it left.对完全无摩擦的情形，用能量审计解释为何滑块返回弹簧时的速率与离开时相同。 [2]

Q10MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 AB阿 AB Diploma-style阿尔伯塔毕业考风格 §5 Power & efficiency (applied)功率与效率（应用） · 20-C2.4k [9 marks][9 分]

An elevator motor lifts a fully loaded car of total mass $1200$ kg at a constant speed of $2.0$ m/s. Take $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$.一台电梯电机以 $2.0$ m/s 的恒定速度提升一节满载、总质量 $1200$ kg 的轿厢。取 $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$。

(a) Find the upward force the cable must exert on the car while it rises at constant speed.求轿厢匀速上升时缆绳必须施加的向上力。 [2]
(b) Find the useful power output of the motor using $P = Fv$.用 $P = Fv$ 求电机的有用功率输出。 [3]
(c) The motor draws $30$ kW of electrical power. Find its efficiency.电机消耗 $30$ kW 的电功率。求其效率。 [2]
(d) Find the rate at which energy is "lost" by the motor, and state what form it takes.求电机"损失"能量的速率，并说明它以何种形式存在。 [2]
Q11MEDIUM中 🇨🇦 AB阿 AB Diploma-style阿尔伯塔毕业考风格 §6 Energy with friction (applied)含摩擦的能量（应用） · 20-C2.6k [9 marks][9 分]

A $70$ kg skier starts from rest at the top of a slope $25$ m high and reaches the bottom moving at $18$ m/s. Take $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$ and the bottom as the reference level.一名 $70$ kg 的滑雪者从一段高 $25$ m 的斜坡顶端静止出发，到达底端时速率为 $18$ m/s。取 $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$，以底端为参考面。

(a) Find the gravitational potential energy of the skier at the top.求滑雪者在顶端的重力势能。 [2]
(b) Find the kinetic energy of the skier at the bottom.求滑雪者在底端的动能。 [2]
(c) Find the energy converted to thermal energy by friction and air resistance during the descent.求下坡过程中摩擦力与空气阻力转化为热能的能量。 [2]
(d) Find the speed the skier would have reached at the bottom if the slope were frictionless, and comment on why it is larger.求若斜坡无摩擦时滑雪者在底端会达到的速率，并说明它为何更大。 [3]
Q12HARD难 🇺🇸 US美 AP-feeder FRQAP 衔接简答题 §7 Energy modeling (roller-coaster)能量建模（过山车） · HS-PS3-1 [10 marks][10 分]

A $500$ kg roller-coaster cart starts from rest at the top of a $35$ m hill on a frictionless track. The track then runs to ground level and up into a vertical loop whose highest point is $20$ m above the ground. Take $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$ and the ground as the reference level.一辆 $500$ kg 的过山车从无摩擦轨道上一座 $35$ m 高的山顶静止出发。轨道随后下到地面，再进入一个竖直环形，环形最高点距地面 $20$ m。取 $g = 9.8\ \text{m/s}^2$，以地面为参考面。

(a) Find the speed of the cart at ground level (bottom of the hill).求小车在地面（山脚）处的速率。 [2]
(b) Find the speed of the cart at the top of the loop.求小车在环顶的速率。 [3]
(c) Find the kinetic energy of the cart at the top of the loop.求小车在环顶的动能。 [2]
(d) Carry out an energy audit at the top of the loop: show that $KE + GPE$ there equals the initial $GPE$ at the top of the hill.在环顶进行能量审计：证明此处的 $KE + GPE$ 等于山顶的初始 $GPE$。 [2]
(e) Explain why a real roller-coaster must start from a hill higher than $35$ m to complete the same loop.解释为何真实的过山车必须从高于 $35$ m 的山坡出发才能完成同样的环形。 [1]
