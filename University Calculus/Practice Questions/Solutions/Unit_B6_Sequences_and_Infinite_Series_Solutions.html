Q1MEDIUMCOREsequence limits and the monotone convergence theorem数列极限与单调收敛定理[8 marks]

Determine convergence of: (a) $a_n=\dfrac{3n^{2}-2n+1}{5n^{2}+4}$; (b) $b_n=\dfrac{(-1)^{n}n}{n^{2}+1}$; (c) $c_1=1$, $c_{n+1}=\sqrt{2+c_n}$ (show monotone, bounded, then find the limit).判断以下数列的收敛性：(a) $a_n=\dfrac{3n^{2}-2n+1}{5n^{2}+4}$；(b) $b_n=\dfrac{(-1)^{n}n}{n^{2}+1}$；(c) $c_1=1$，$c_{n+1}=\sqrt{2+c_n}$（证明单调有界，再求极限）。

Answers:答案：  (a) converges to收敛至 $\tfrac{3}{5}$  ·  (b) converges to收敛至 $0$  ·  (c) converges to收敛至 $2$

(a) Divide by $n^{2}$(a) 分子分母除以 $n^{2}$ M1·A1

Divide every term in numerator and denominator by $n^{2}$: (M1)将分子与分母的每一项均除以 $n^{2}$：(M1)

$$ a_n = \frac{3 - 2/n + 1/n^{2}}{5 + 4/n^{2}} \longrightarrow \frac{3-0+0}{5+0} = \frac{3}{5}. $$

The sequence converges to $\dfrac{3}{5}$. (A1)该数列收敛至 $\dfrac{3}{5}$。(A1)

(b) Squeeze between $-1/n$ and $1/n$(b) 用 $-1/n$ 和 $1/n$ 夹逼 M1·A1

For all $n \ge 1$, $n^{2}+1 > n^{2}$, so $|b_n| = \dfrac{n}{n^{2}+1} < \dfrac{n}{n^{2}} = \dfrac{1}{n}$. (M1)对所有 $n \ge 1$，$n^{2}+1 > n^{2}$，故 $|b_n| = \dfrac{n}{n^{2}+1} < \dfrac{n}{n^{2}} = \dfrac{1}{n}$。(M1)

Thus $-\dfrac{1}{n} < b_n < \dfrac{1}{n}$. Since $\pm 1/n \to 0$, the squeeze theorem gives $b_n \to 0$. The alternating sign does not prevent convergence; it is absorbed into the squeeze. (A1)因此 $-\dfrac{1}{n} < b_n < \dfrac{1}{n}$。由于 $\pm 1/n \to 0$，夹逼定理给出 $b_n \to 0$。交替符号不影响收敛性，已被夹逼吸收。(A1)

(c) Increasing and bounded, then solve the fixed-point equation(c) 单调有界，再解不动点方程 M1·M1·A1·A1

Increasing.单调递增。 Compute $c_2 = \sqrt{2+1} = \sqrt{3} \approx 1.73 > 1 = c_1$. Suppose $c_n > c_{n-1}$; then $c_{n+1} = \sqrt{2+c_n} > \sqrt{2+c_{n-1}} = c_n$. By induction $\{c_n\}$ is increasing. (M1)计算 $c_2 = \sqrt{2+1} = \sqrt{3} \approx 1.73 > 1 = c_1$。假设 $c_n > c_{n-1}$，则 $c_{n+1} = \sqrt{2+c_n} > \sqrt{2+c_{n-1}} = c_n$。由归纳法，$\{c_n\}$ 单调递增。(M1)

Bounded above by $3$.有上界 $3$。 Base: $c_1 = 1 < 3$. Inductive step: if $c_n < 3$ then $c_{n+1} = \sqrt{2+c_n} < \sqrt{2+3} = \sqrt{5} < 3$. (M1)基础情形：$c_1 = 1 < 3$。归纳步骤：若 $c_n < 3$，则 $c_{n+1} = \sqrt{2+c_n} < \sqrt{2+3} = \sqrt{5} < 3$。(M1)

By the Monotone Convergence Theorem, $L = \displaystyle\lim_{n\to\infty} c_n$ exists. (A1)由单调收敛定理，$L = \displaystyle\lim_{n\to\infty} c_n$ 存在。(A1)

Finding $L$.求 $L$。 Taking the limit on both sides of $c_{n+1} = \sqrt{2+c_n}$ gives $L = \sqrt{2+L}$, so $L^{2} = 2 + L$, i.e. $L^{2} - L - 2 = 0$, which factors as $(L-2)(L+1) = 0$. Since $c_n \ge 1 > 0$ for all $n$, we discard $L = -1$ and conclude $L = 2$. (A1)对 $c_{n+1} = \sqrt{2+c_n}$ 两边取极限得 $L = \sqrt{2+L}$，故 $L^{2} = 2 + L$，即 $L^{2} - L - 2 = 0$，因式分解得 $(L-2)(L+1) = 0$。由于对所有 $n$ 均有 $c_n \ge 1 > 0$，舍去 $L = -1$，得 $L = 2$。(A1)

Insight.解题要点。 The Monotone Convergence Theorem licenses you to take the limit of the recurrence only after you have proved convergence independently. Taking the limit first and solving $L = \sqrt{2+L}$ is circular unless you already know $L$ exists. Showing "increasing and bounded above" is the key step; the fixed-point algebra is just the computation at the end.单调收敛定理允许对递推关系取极限，但前提是已独立证明了收敛性。若未先证明 $L$ 存在便直接解 $L = \sqrt{2+L}$，则推理是循环的。证明"单调递增且有上界"是核心步骤，不动点代数仅是最后的计算。
Q2MEDIUMCOREgeometric series and the nth-term divergence test等比级数与第n项发散判别法[8 marks]

Convergence of: (a) $\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}\tfrac{5}{3^{n}}$; (b) $\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\tfrac{2^{n}+3^{n}}{6^{n}}$; (c) $\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\tfrac{n^{2}}{n^{2}+1}$.判断以下级数的收敛性：(a) $\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}\tfrac{5}{3^{n}}$；(b) $\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\tfrac{2^{n}+3^{n}}{6^{n}}$；(c) $\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\tfrac{n^{2}}{n^{2}+1}$。

Answers:答案：  (a) converges to收敛至 $\tfrac{15}{2}$  ·  (b) converges to收敛至 $\tfrac{3}{2}$  ·  (c) diverges发散

(a) Geometric series with $r = 1/3$(a) 公比 $r = 1/3$ 的等比级数 M1·A1

The series has first term $a = 5$ and common ratio $r = 1/3$, with $|r| = 1/3 < 1$. (M1) By the geometric series formula,该级数首项 $a = 5$，公比 $r = 1/3$，$|r| = 1/3 < 1$。(M1) 由等比级数公式，

$$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{5}{3^{n}} = \frac{5}{1 - 1/3} = \frac{5}{2/3} = \frac{15}{2}. \quad \text{(A1)} $$

(b) Split into two geometric series(b) 拆分为两个等比级数 M1·A1·A1

Write $\dfrac{2^{n}+3^{n}}{6^{n}} = \left(\dfrac{2}{6}\right)^{n} + \left(\dfrac{3}{6}\right)^{n} = \left(\dfrac{1}{3}\right)^{n} + \left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^{n}$. (M1)将 $\dfrac{2^{n}+3^{n}}{6^{n}} = \left(\dfrac{2}{6}\right)^{n} + \left(\dfrac{3}{6}\right)^{n} = \left(\dfrac{1}{3}\right)^{n} + \left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^{n}$。(M1)

Both geometric series start at $n=1$ and have ratio inside $(-1,1)$:两个等比级数均从 $n=1$ 开始，公比均在 $(-1,1)$ 内：

$$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^{n} = \frac{1/3}{1-1/3} = \frac{1}{2}, \qquad \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{n} = \frac{1/2}{1-1/2} = 1. \quad \text{(A1 each)} $$

The total sum is $\dfrac{1}{2} + 1 = \dfrac{3}{2}$. (A1)两者之和为 $\dfrac{1}{2} + 1 = \dfrac{3}{2}$。(A1)

(c) Nth-term divergence test(c) 第n项发散判别法 M1·A1·R1

Compute the term limit: $\displaystyle\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{n^{2}}{n^{2}+1} = \lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{1}{1+1/n^{2}} = 1 \ne 0$. (M1)计算通项极限：$\displaystyle\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{n^{2}}{n^{2}+1} = \lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{1}{1+1/n^{2}} = 1 \ne 0$。(M1)

Because the terms do not tend to zero, the $n$th-term divergence test immediately gives divergence. (A1) The series diverges. (R1)由于通项不趋于零，第 $n$ 项发散判别法直接得出发散结论。(A1) 该级数发散。(R1)

Insight.解题要点。 The nth-term test can only prove divergence; it never proves convergence. Here it fires at the very first check. Always apply it before any harder test: if $a_n \not\to 0$, stop. Part (b) shows that when a series looks like a ratio of exponentials, break it into single-base geometric pieces.第 $n$ 项判别法只能证明发散，不能证明收敛。本题在第一步检验时便已触发。始终先于其他复杂判别法使用该法：若 $a_n \not\to 0$，即可停止。(b) 表明，当级数形如指数之比时，应将其拆分为各自基底的等比级数。
Q3MEDIUMCOREtelescoping series via partial fractions利用部分分式求裂项级数[5 marks]

For $\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n(n+2)}$: (a) find the partial fraction decomposition; (b) collapse the $N$th partial sum and evaluate the series.对于 $\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n(n+2)}$：(a) 求部分分式分解；(b) 化简第 $N$ 个部分和并求级数的精确值。

Answers:答案：  (a) $A = \tfrac{1}{2},\ B = -\tfrac{1}{2}$  ·  (b) $\displaystyle\sum = \dfrac{3}{4}$

(a) Partial fractions(a) 部分分式 M1·A1

Write $\dfrac{1}{n(n+2)} = \dfrac{A}{n} + \dfrac{B}{n+2}$, so $1 = A(n+2) + Bn$. (M1)设 $\dfrac{1}{n(n+2)} = \dfrac{A}{n} + \dfrac{B}{n+2}$，则 $1 = A(n+2) + Bn$。(M1)

Setting $n = 0$: $1 = 2A$, so $A = \tfrac{1}{2}$. Setting $n = -2$: $1 = -2B$, so $B = -\tfrac{1}{2}$. (A1)令 $n = 0$：$1 = 2A$，故 $A = \tfrac{1}{2}$。令 $n = -2$：$1 = -2B$，故 $B = -\tfrac{1}{2}$。(A1)

(b) Telescope and take the limit(b) 裂项相消后取极限 M1·A1·A1

Write the $N$th partial sum using the decomposition: (M1)利用分解式写出第 $N$ 个部分和：(M1)

$$ S_N = \frac{1}{2}\sum_{n=1}^{N}\left(\frac{1}{n} - \frac{1}{n+2}\right). $$

List the first few terms of the expanded sum:展开前几项：

$$ \left(\frac{1}{1}-\frac{1}{3}\right)+\left(\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{4}\right)+\left(\frac{1}{3}-\frac{1}{5}\right)+\cdots+\left(\frac{1}{N}-\frac{1}{N+2}\right). $$

Every interior term $\tfrac{1}{k}$ (for $3 \le k \le N$) is cancelled by the later term $-\tfrac{1}{k}$. The surviving terms are $\tfrac{1}{1}$, $\tfrac{1}{2}$, $-\tfrac{1}{N+1}$, $-\tfrac{1}{N+2}$, giving (A1)所有内部项 $\tfrac{1}{k}$（$3 \le k \le N$）均与后续项 $-\tfrac{1}{k}$ 相消。保留的项为 $\tfrac{1}{1}$、$\tfrac{1}{2}$、$-\tfrac{1}{N+1}$、$-\tfrac{1}{N+2}$，得 (A1)

$$ S_N = \frac{1}{2}\left(1 + \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{N+1} - \frac{1}{N+2}\right). $$

As $N \to \infty$, the last two terms vanish:当 $N \to \infty$ 时，最后两项趋于零：

$$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n(n+2)} = \frac{1}{2}\left(1+\frac{1}{2}\right) = \frac{1}{2}\cdot\frac{3}{2} = \frac{3}{4}. \quad \text{(A1)} $$
Insight.解题要点。 A telescoping series can only be evaluated exactly once you identify which terms survive. For a skip of $2$ (i.e. $1/n - 1/(n+2)$), two "first" terms and two "last" terms survive instead of the usual one of each. Always write out the pattern explicitly rather than guessing which terms cancel.裂项级数只有在确定哪些项保留之后才能精确求值。对于间距为 $2$ 的情形（即 $1/n - 1/(n+2)$），有两个"首项"和两个"末项"保留，而非各一项。应始终明确写出规律，而非猜测哪些项相消。
Q4HARDCOREintegral test and p-series积分判别法与p-级数[7 marks]

Convergence with justification: (a) $\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\tfrac{1}{n^{3/2}}$; (b) $\displaystyle\sum_{n=2}^{\infty}\tfrac{1}{n\ln n}$ by the integral test; (c) convergence values of $p$ for $\displaystyle\sum\tfrac{1}{n^{p}}$.附完整论证判断收敛性：(a) $\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\tfrac{1}{n^{3/2}}$；(b) 用积分判别法处理 $\displaystyle\sum_{n=2}^{\infty}\tfrac{1}{n\ln n}$；(c) 确定 $\displaystyle\sum\tfrac{1}{n^{p}}$ 收敛的 $p$ 值范围。

Answers:答案：  (a) converges收敛  ·  (b) diverges发散  ·  (c) converges iff $p > 1$; diverges if $p \le 1$当且仅当 $p > 1$ 时收敛；$p \le 1$ 时发散

(a) p-series test(a) p-级数判别法 A1·R1

This is a $p$-series with $p = \tfrac{3}{2} > 1$. (A1) By the $p$-series theorem, it converges. (R1)这是 $p = \tfrac{3}{2} > 1$ 的 $p$-级数。(A1) 由 $p$-级数定理，该级数收敛。(R1)

(b) Integral test on $f(x) = 1/(x\ln x)$(b) 对 $f(x) = 1/(x\ln x)$ 应用积分判别法 M1·M1·A1·A1

Verify hypotheses.验证前提条件。 For $x \ge 2$, $f(x) = \dfrac{1}{x\ln x}$ is positive ($\ln x > 0$ for $x > 1$), continuous, and decreasing (both $x$ and $\ln x$ are increasing, so their product is increasing, and its reciprocal is decreasing). (M1)当 $x \ge 2$ 时，$f(x) = \dfrac{1}{x\ln x}$ 为正值（$x > 1$ 时 $\ln x > 0$），连续，且单调递减（$x$ 与 $\ln x$ 均递增，故其乘积递增，倒数递减）。(M1)

Evaluate the improper integral.计算广义积分。 Substitute $u = \ln x$, $du = dx/x$: (M1)令 $u = \ln x$，$du = dx/x$：(M1)

$$ \int_{2}^{\infty}\frac{dx}{x\ln x} = \int_{\ln 2}^{\infty}\frac{du}{u} = \Big[\ln u\Big]_{\ln 2}^{\infty} = \infty. \quad \text{(A1)} $$

Since the integral diverges, the integral test gives: $\displaystyle\sum_{n=2}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{n\ln n}$ diverges. (A1)由于广义积分发散，积分判别法给出：$\displaystyle\sum_{n=2}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{n\ln n}$ 发散。(A1)

(c) The p-series threshold(c) p-级数的临界值 A1

$\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{n^{p}}$ converges if and only if $p > 1$. It diverges for $p \le 1$, including the boundary case $p = 1$ (the harmonic series), which diverges as established by the integral test since $\int_1^\infty x^{-1}\,dx = \infty$. (A1)$\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{n^{p}}$ 当且仅当 $p > 1$ 时收敛。当 $p \le 1$ 时发散，包括边界情形 $p = 1$（调和级数），由积分判别法可知 $\int_1^\infty x^{-1}\,dx = \infty$ 故发散。(A1)

Insight.解题要点。 The integral test turns a series question into a calculus question, but it demands three checked hypotheses before it is valid. The substitution $u = \ln x$ in (b) is the same move that unlocks many "log in the denominator" integrals. Note that $1/(n\ln n)$ decays faster than $1/n$ yet still diverges: decay alone is not enough, and the $p$-series boundary $p = 1$ sits strictly between convergent and divergent behavior.积分判别法将级数问题转化为微积分问题，但在使用前须验证三个前提条件。(b) 中的换元 $u = \ln x$ 是处理"分母含对数"的积分的标准技巧。注意 $1/(n\ln n)$ 衰减比 $1/n$ 快，但仍发散：仅凭衰减速度不足以判断，$p$-级数边界 $p = 1$ 恰好处于收敛与发散行为之间。

Q5HARDPROOFderiving the geometric series sum formula推导等比级数求和公式[8 marks]

With $S_N = \displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{N} r^{n}$: (a) prove $S_N = \dfrac{1-r^{N+1}}{1-r}$; (b) prove $\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} r^{n} = \dfrac{1}{1-r}$ when $|r| < 1$, and explain why the formula fails for $|r| \ge 1$.设 $S_N = \displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{N} r^{n}$：(a) 证明 $S_N = \dfrac{1-r^{N+1}}{1-r}$；(b) 证明当 $|r| < 1$ 时 $\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} r^{n} = \dfrac{1}{1-r}$，并解释当 $|r| \ge 1$ 时公式失效的原因。

Answers:答案：  (a) $S_N = \dfrac{1-r^{N+1}}{1-r}$ (for $r \ne 1$)（$r \ne 1$ 时）  ·  (b) $\dfrac{1}{1-r}$ (and the formula fails when $r^{N+1}\not\to 0$)（当 $r^{N+1}\not\to 0$ 时公式失效）

(a) The multiply-and-subtract trick(a) 乘以公比再相减的技巧 M1·M1·A1·A1

Write out $S_N$ and $rS_N$ aligned by powers of $r$: (M1)将 $S_N$ 与 $rS_N$ 按 $r$ 的幂次对齐写出：(M1)

$$ S_N = 1 + r + r^{2} + \cdots + r^{N}, $$ $$ rS_N = \phantom{1 + {}}r + r^{2} + \cdots + r^{N} + r^{N+1}. $$

Subtract: (M1)相减：(M1)

$$ S_N - rS_N = 1 - r^{N+1}, \quad\text{so}\quad (1-r)S_N = 1-r^{N+1}. \quad \text{(A1)} $$

Since $r \ne 1$ (which holds for $|r| < 1$ in part (b), and more generally whenever $r \ne 1$), divide both sides by $1-r$:由于 $r \ne 1$（(b) 中 $|r| < 1$ 保证，更一般地在 $r \ne 1$ 时均成立），两边除以 $1-r$：

$$ S_N = \frac{1 - r^{N+1}}{1-r}. \quad \text{(A1)} $$

(b) Taking the limit and diagnosing failure for $|r| \ge 1$(b) 取极限并分析 $|r| \ge 1$ 时的失效情形 M1·A1·R1·A1

When $|r| < 1$: we have $|r^{N+1}| = |r|^{N+1} \to 0$ as $N \to \infty$, because $|r| < 1$ implies the geometric sequence $|r|^{N+1}$ is decreasing to zero. (M1)当 $|r| < 1$ 时：$|r^{N+1}| = |r|^{N+1} \to 0$（$N \to \infty$），因为 $|r| < 1$ 意味着等比数列 $|r|^{N+1}$ 单调递减趋于零。(M1)

Therefore (A1)从而 (A1)

$$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} r^{n} = \lim_{N\to\infty} S_N = \lim_{N\to\infty}\frac{1-r^{N+1}}{1-r} = \frac{1-0}{1-r} = \frac{1}{1-r}. $$

When $|r| = 1$: either $r = 1$ (and the formula for $S_N$ was derived by dividing by $1-r$, which is zero, so the derivation breaks down), or $|r| = 1$ with $r \ne 1$ (so $|r^{N+1}| = 1$ for all $N$, and $r^{N+1}$ oscillates without tending to zero). (R1)当 $|r| = 1$ 时：若 $r = 1$，则 $S_N$ 的推导中需除以 $1-r = 0$，推导失效；若 $|r| = 1$ 且 $r \ne 1$，则 $|r^{N+1}| = 1$ 对所有 $N$ 成立，$r^{N+1}$ 振荡而不趋于零。(R1)

When $|r| > 1$: $|r^{N+1}| = |r|^{N+1} \to \infty$, so $S_N$ diverges. In both cases $|r| \ge 1$, the limit $\displaystyle\lim_{N\to\infty}S_N$ does not exist, and the sum formula $\dfrac{1}{1-r}$ has no meaning as the value of the series. (A1)当 $|r| > 1$ 时：$|r^{N+1}| = |r|^{N+1} \to \infty$，故 $S_N$ 发散。两种情形下（$|r| \ge 1$），$\displaystyle\lim_{N\to\infty}S_N$ 均不存在，公式 $\dfrac{1}{1-r}$ 作为级数和无任何意义。(A1)

Insight.解题要点。 The derivation is algorithmic: multiply by $r$, subtract, divide by $1-r$. The convergence step is separate: it depends on whether $r^{N+1}\to 0$. The formula $1/(1-r)$ is valid for $|r|<1$; plugging in $|r|\ge 1$ gives a finite-looking number that is not the sum of the series. For example, $r=2$ gives $-1$, which is meaningless as a sum of positive terms.推导是机械化的：乘以 $r$，相减，除以 $1-r$。收敛性的论证是独立的：取决于 $r^{N+1}$ 是否趋于零。公式 $1/(1-r)$ 仅在 $|r|<1$ 时有效；代入 $|r|\ge 1$ 可得看似有限的数，但它并非级数的和。例如 $r=2$ 给出 $-1$，作为正项和毫无意义。
Q6HARDPROOFproving the nth-term divergence test and the divergence of the harmonic series证明第n项发散判别法与调和级数的发散性[9 marks]

(a) Prove: if $\sum a_n$ converges, then $a_n \to 0$, and state the contrapositive. (b) Prove $\sum 1/n$ diverges using the integral test.(a) 证明：若 $\sum a_n$ 收敛，则 $a_n \to 0$，并陈述其逆否命题。(b) 用积分判别法证明 $\sum 1/n$ 发散。

Answers:答案：  (a) $a_N = S_N - S_{N-1} \to S - S = 0$; contrapositive is the divergence test逆否命题即为发散判别法  ·  (b) $\int_1^\infty x^{-1}\,dx = \infty$, so $\sum 1/n$ diverges故 $\sum 1/n$ 发散

(a) The nth-term condition and its contrapositive(a) 第n项条件及其逆否命题 M1·M1·A1·R1·A1

$$ a_N = S_N - S_{N-1}. $$

$$ \lim_{N\to\infty} a_N = \lim_{N\to\infty}(S_N - S_{N-1}) = S - S = 0. $$

$$ \int_{1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{x}\,dx = \lim_{t\to\infty}\int_{1}^{t}\frac{1}{x}\,dx = \lim_{t\to\infty}[\ln x]_{1}^{t} = \lim_{t\to\infty}(\ln t - 0) = \infty. \quad \text{(A1)} $$

Insight.解题要点。 The $n$th-term test can only prove divergence; here the harmonic series passes the term test (since $1/n \to 0$) yet diverges. Q8HARDAPPLIEDratio and root tests: test selection and full justification比值判别法与根式判别法：判别法选择与完整论证[9 marks]

(a) Ratio test on $n^{3}/3^{n}$(a) 对 $n^{3}/3^{n}$ 用比值判别法 M1·A1·R1

The series has both a power of $n$ and an exponential, making the ratio test natural. Let $a_n = n^{3}/3^{n}$. (M1)该级数同时含 $n$ 的幂次与指数，适合用比值判别法。设 $a_n = n^{3}/3^{n}$。(M1)

$$ \frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n} = \frac{(n+1)^{3}}{3^{n+1}}\cdot\frac{3^{n}}{n^{3}} = \frac{1}{3}\left(\frac{n+1}{n}\right)^{3} = \frac{1}{3}\left(1+\frac{1}{n}\right)^{3} \longrightarrow \frac{1}{3}\cdot 1 = \frac{1}{3}. $$

(b) Ratio test on $4^{n}n!/(2n)!$(b) 对 $4^{n}n!/(2n)!$ 用比值判别法 M1·A1·R1

Factorials respond well to the ratio test. Let $a_n = 4^{n}n!/(2n)!$. (M1)阶乘适合用比值判别法。设 $a_n = 4^{n}n!/(2n)!$。(M1)

$$ \frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n} = \frac{4^{n+1}(n+1)!}{(2n+2)!}\cdot\frac{(2n)!}{4^{n}n!} = \frac{4(n+1)}{(2n+2)(2n+1)} = \frac{4(n+1)}{2(n+1)(2n+1)} = \frac{2}{2n+1} \longrightarrow 0. $$

(c) Root test on $\bigl((2n+1)/(3n+2)\bigr)^{n}$(c) 对 $\bigl((2n+1)/(3n+2)\bigr)^{n}$ 用根式判别法 M1·A1·R1

The $n$th power makes the root test the natural choice. Let $a_n = \left(\dfrac{2n+1}{3n+2}\right)^{n}$. (M1)含 $n$ 次幂，根式判别法是自然选择。设 $a_n = \left(\dfrac{2n+1}{3n+2}\right)^{n}$。(M1)

Since $L = \tfrac{2}{3} < 1$, the root test gives convergence. (A1·R1)由于 $L = \tfrac{2}{3} < 1$，根式判别法给出收敛结论。(A1·R1)

Insight.解题要点。 The ratio test excels for factorials and products of exponentials; the root test excels for $n$th-power expressions. To use the bound, solve the inequality $(N+1)^p > 1/\varepsilon$ for the appropriate power $p$; here $p=3$ gives a very accurate approximation very quickly. Contrast with $p$-series $\sum 1/n$, where you would need $N > 200$ terms for the same accuracy.交错级数余项定理给出单侧误差界：$S$ 位于 $S_N$ 与 $S_{N+1}$ 之间（与 $S_{N+1}$ 同侧）。这比大多数误差估计强得多。使用该界时，求解不等式 $(N+1)^p > 1/\varepsilon$（取对应的幂次 $p$）；这里 $p=3$ 使得近似精度很快达标。对比 $p$-级数 $\sum 1/n$，若要达到同等精度需要超过 $200$ 项。
